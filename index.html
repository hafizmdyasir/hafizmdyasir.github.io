Intro

Mohammad Yasir is pursuing M. Sc. in Physics from the Indian Institute of Technology. His passion for literature and skill with words have lent him a unique voice in the realms of poetry and prose alike.
Alongside his poetic endeavours, Yasir has also authored online content on various topics, both academic and otherwise that has appeared across websites like HubPages and Medium. He is also involved in a number of android app development projects.

Apps

WhatsAppDirect

WhatsApp Direct is a minimal Android application that helps you send WhatsApp messages to unsaved contacts without needing to save their number to the phone book first. The app leverages official WhatsApp API to open the chat window of a particular number directly.

Books

Alliterations

Hopes and dreams reside within the pages of Alliterations - a collection of thirty poems penned in response to the January Journal challenge. These sweet and simple rhymes capture the struggles and joys that define a fulfilling and cherished life.
