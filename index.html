Intro

Hi. I am Mohammad Yasir, a Physics graduate from the University of Delhi, currently pursuing M. Sc. in Physics from the Indian Institute of Technology. Alongside my scientific interests, I am also passionate about literature and poetry.
I have authored online content on various topics, both academic and otherwise, and it has appeared across websites like HubPages and Medium. I also enjoy being involved in a number of Android app development projects as a hobby.

Writing

1. Book of Interesting Physics

Physics can be intriguing, perplexing, paradoxical, impossible, and above all, fun!
The Book of Interesting Physics is a repository on GitHub forked from collegefishes's account. I am attempting to contribute paradoxes and questions that live up to the name of the book. The idea is to bring Physics to life in front of the reader and demonstrate its beauty in simple terms.

2. Alliterations

Hopes and dreams reside within the pages of Alliterations - a collection of thirty poems penned in response to the January Journal challenge. These sweet and simple rhymes capture the struggles and joys that define a fulfilling and cherished life.
